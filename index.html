11 Years Of Experience Within The Machinery Industry

Profoundly completed a decade in the field of Machinery and Industrial Solutions, YES Machinery has become the synonym for quality, innovation and service. Founded in the year 2014 in UAE, YES Machinery set about with the vision to bring a remarkable change in the Machinery supply sector. More than machines, we aim at offering complete solutions for all your industrial requirements.

We’re proud that from a very humble beginning, YES Machinery has taken its leap to a premium machinery supplier in the region representing the World’s #1 brands across steel fabrication, metal working, automation, industrial solutions and cleaning, material handing, flooring solutions and vehicle organization. YES Machinery ensures a hands-on approach by closely interacting with the customers and understanding their requirements and delivering the best possible solutions.

Been active in this market for 11+ years, YES Machinery continuously update their product range to cater to the evolving needs by embracing new technologies, automation as well as robotics and set up a super strong Service team capable of offering end-to-end solutions and support right from installation and operational training to after sales support including spare parts.

At YES Machinery, we believe that the most important aspect of our success is having been able to have a long-term relationship with our customers. More than just delivering a machine one time, we constantly keep a connection with our customers to check on the status of the machines.

With its decade long experience, YES Machinery has been the chosen partner for many industries from construction to manufacturing to oil & gas and aviation. As we celebrate this important milestone, YES Machinery looks forward to serving the customers more efficiently and effectively and empowering the industries with our smart solutions.
